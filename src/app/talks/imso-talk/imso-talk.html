Outer Billiards

1. Fix a polygon $P$ in the plane and choose a point $x$. 2. Two support lines to $P$ pass through $x$. 3. Orient so that $P$ stays on the left. 4. Reflect $x$ through the forward vertex of $P$. $T(x):=y$. 5. Continue: $T^2(x)=z$, and so on.

Discontinuity Set

We exclude (iterated preimages of) side extensions from the domain of $T$.
